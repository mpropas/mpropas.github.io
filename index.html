Chartered Enterprise Risk Analyst

A Chartered Enterprise Risk Analyst (CERA) has deep Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) expertise to understand an organization’s risk profile.

The CERA qualification encompasses curriculum that combines actuarial science with the theoretical, practical and professional principles of ERM. It applies both qualitative and quantitative insight to ERM and equips risk management professionals to empower better business decisions.

Associate of the Society of Actuaries

An Associate of the Society of Actuaries (ASA) has demonstrated knowledge of the fundamental techniques for modeling and managing risk.

The ASA has learned the basic methods of applying those concepts and techniques to common problems involving uncertain future events, especially those with financial implications. The Associate has also completed a professionalism course covering the professional code of conduct and the importance of adherence to recognized standards of practice.
